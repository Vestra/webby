Ruben Gonzalez

About Ruben

Ruben Gonzalez is current foster youth apart of the #FosterCode program, a free and immersive 10-week course with a cohert of 20 transition-age foster youth for the start of a career in computer science.


Ruben Gonzalez, was born February 22, 1999 in Bellflower, California.


From a very young age Ruben questioned the world around him, which later continued throughout his life.


Inevitably, due to poor behavior and academic performance in school, his mother decided to send him to live in Mexico with his father's side of his family.


He began living by himself in a small room behind his grandmother's home in Ensenada, Mexico.


His father would work during the week as a taxi driver in a neighboring city, only visiting him on weekends

His inconsistent childhood—loss of family, friends, and way of life—eventually led him into depression at the age of 13.


Ruben stuggled with his depression, which eventually became severe depression, he then began eating and drinking less water, stopped attending school, and would stay indoors without sunlight for days at a time.


Ruben later overcame his depression by forming new habits—reading, meditating, and exercising—which pulled him into a growth mindset.
